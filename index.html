Nosh

A modern recipe app with a spoonful of personal safety. Nosh is a new recipe app that has a hidden helpline nestled among its screens, for domestic violence victims.
‍
The goal of Nosh is simple: to help everyone enjoy a delicious home cooked meal. In addition, Nosh focuses on survivors of domestic violence abuse, or those who might be in a victim situation and need help reaching out if they are in trouble.
